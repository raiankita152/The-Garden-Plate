A Selection Of Flavours Crafted For You

Crispy Calamari Rings

Tender squid rings lightly coated in seasoned batter and fried until perfectly golden and crispy. Served with a creamy garlic aioli dip for a delightful balance of crunch and flavor.

Caramel Macchiato

A luxurious blend of steamed milk and rich espresso, layered with smooth caramel sauce for a perfectly balanced sweetness. Finished with a caramel drizzle, creating a creamy and indulgent coffee experience.

Crispy Pesarattu

A traditional South Indian crepe made from green gram (moong dal), cooked until golden and crispy for a delightful crunch. Served with fresh coconut chutney and spicy ginger chutney, offering a perfect blend of flavor and authenticity.

Stuffed Chicken Roll

Tender chicken breast rolled and stuffed with creamy ricotta cheese, fresh herbs, and aromatic seasonings, then roasted to perfection. Served with garlic butter sauce and seasonal vegetables for a rich and elegant dining experience
