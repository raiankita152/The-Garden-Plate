About Us

About The Garden Plate

A Taste of Togetherness

At The Garden Plate, we believe that the best moments in life are shared around the table. Combining fresh ingredients, thoughtfully crafted dishes, and a warm nature-inspired ambiance, we create a dining experience where every meal becomes a celebration of flavor, connection, and unforgettable memories.

Our Story

The Garden Plate was created with a simple idea to offer a dining experience where nature, flavor, and hospitality come together. Inspired by the beauty of garden surroundings, we serve thoughtfully crafted dishes made from fresh ingredients and timeless recipes. Every meal is prepared with care, every guest is welcomed like family, and every visit is designed to leave lasting memories. Here, dining is not just about food it's about sharing moments that matter.

OUR MISSION

To create exceptional dining experiences by serving fresh, thoughtfully prepared dishes while delivering genuine hospitality in a nature-inspired setting.

OUR VISION

To become the preferred destination for guests seeking unforgettable food, meaningful connections, and an elegant dining experience rooted in sustainability and excellence.

OUR VALUES

We believe in serving fresh, high-quality food with warmth and care. Guided by sustainability, excellence, and a passion for hospitality, we strive to create memorable experiences in every visit.

Meet Our Culinary Masters

Chef Arjun Mehta

With 18 years of experience in fine dining, Chef Arjun Mehta has mastered Italian and European cuisine. He specializes in creating luxurious dishes using premium ingredients and modern cooking techniques. His expertise lies in truffle-based dishes, gourmet tasting menus, and artistic food presentation.

Recipient of the Culinary Excellence Award 2022 and International Chef of the Year 2021. His innovative menus have earned widespread recognition in the fine dining industry. He was also honored with the Best Fine Dining Menu Award 2023.

Chef Ananya Sharma

Chef Ananya Sharma brings 12 years of culinary experience with a focus on Mediterranean and fusion cuisine. She is known for blending traditional flavors with contemporary cooking styles. Her expertise includes healthy gourmet dishes, creative salads, and innovative menu development.

Winner of the Rising Culinary Star Award 2018 and Best Innovative Dish Award 2020. Her creative approach to fine dining has earned praise from both guests and industry professionals. She also received the Excellence in Culinary Arts Award 2022.

Chef Rohan Verma

With 10 years of experience, Chef Ethan specializes in pastry arts and dessert creation. He combines classic French techniques with modern dessert innovation to craft unforgettable sweet experiences. His expertise includes artisan cakes, chocolate creations, and premium plated desserts.

Awarded Best Pastry Chef 2021 and Dessert Innovation Award 2023 for his exceptional creations. His desserts are celebrated for their creativity, flavor, and presentation. He also earned a Gold Medal in a prestigious culinar competition in 2022.
